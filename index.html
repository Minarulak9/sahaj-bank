Distributor

As a distributor with Sahaj Bank, you have the unique opportunity to expand your business and maximize your reach by creating an unlimited number of retailers under your network. Here's a short instruction guide on how to create retailers under your distributorship:

  • Log in to your Sahaj Bank distributor account using your credentials.
  • Navigate to the "Retailer Management" section or a similar option specifically designated for creating retailers.
  • Click on the "Create Retailer" button or a similar prompt to initiate the process.
  • Fill in the required information for each retailer, including their name, contact details, and any additional necessary details such as address or business registration.
  • Assign a unique retailer ID or code to each retailer for easy identification and tracking.
  • Set the retailer's credit limit or transaction limits, if applicable, based on your business agreement or arrangement with them.
  • Once you have entered all the necessary details, click on the "Submit" or "Create" button to finalize the retailer creation process.
  • Repeat steps 4 to 7 for each additional retailer you wish to create under your distributorship.

Remember, as a distributor, you have the responsibility to oversee and support the retailers under your network. You can provide them with necessary training, guidance, and marketing materials to help them succeed in their respective businesses.

By creating an unlimited number of retailers under your distributorship, you can expand your business network, increase your revenue streams, and contribute to the growth of Sahaj Bank's B2B ecosystem.

Note: The exact steps and options may vary depending on the specific interface and features provided by Sahaj Bank's distributor portal. It is recommended to refer to the official documentation or contact Sahaj Bank's support team for detailed instructions tailored to your account.
